Hi!

I'm Daniel, a game programmer with experience in many areas of programming - most recently, a lot of C#, Unity, and some C++. I have a bachelors degree in computer science, and a love for making games. Whenever I come across a topic I'm very interested in and I think other people will find useful, I will make informational/tutorial videos to help solidify my knowledge and hopefully help some people along the way. I also participate in many game jams,and have been prototyping a couple of larger projects.

This page holds a summary of many of these projects and each project has its own page where I explain more about it. Go take a look! :)
